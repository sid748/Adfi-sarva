Frequently Asked Questions

You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers

How is AdFi different from a traditional bank loan?

AdFi is built for digital businesses. While banks often require physical collateral, extensive paperwork, personal guarantees, and multi-week underwriting. AdFi leverages real-time business data (like revenue, ad performance, and bank) to assess your growth potential. Key differences:

  • Non-dilutive, performance-based financing—no equity dilution or personal guarantees.
  • Speed—apply and get funded in days, not weeks or months.
  • Flexibility—repayment aligns with your revenue; no rigid monthly principal + interest obligations.
  • Modern underwriting based on CAC efficiency, LTV, ROAS.

What kind of companies does AdFi serve?

AdFi supports growth-stage, digitally-native companies that rely heavily on paid marketing to acquire customers. Our sweet spot includes:

  • DTC (Direct-to-Consumer) brands scaling via paid channels like Meta, TikTok, or Google.
  • SaaS and subscription businesses with strong retention and LTV-to-CAC ratio.
  • E-commerce companies with recurring customers and scalable CAC funnels
  • B2B digital platforms where revenue is tied to predictable user acquisition pipelines.
  • We work with companies generating at least $100K/month in revenue (or equivalent MRR) and who can demonstrate efficient customer acquisition with real payback.

How does your technology work?

AdFi’s platform connects directly to your real-time revenue, bank, and advertising data, which allows us to assess your application quickly and more accurately than traditional lenders.

  • We analyze your CAC payback curve, RoAS trends, revenue seasonality, retention, and LTV in minutes.
  • Our proprietary underwriting engine builds a dynamic repayment structure that flexes with your performance.
  • Borrowers can track funding limits, repayments, and performance metrics from a single dashboard.
  • Our system continuously monitors your data post-funding and flagging opportunities to unlock more capital or adjust repayment terms.
  • This tech-first approach enables better credit decisions and faster capital deployment—with zero friction.

How will using AdFi help me avoid diluting my equity?

  • Every dollar you raise via equity dilutes your ownership. With AdFi, you raise growth capital without giving up a single share.
  • Instead of selling equity to fund marketing or customer acquisition, you use AdFi to front-load that marketing spend. You repay us only after that spend starts generating results, preserving your cap table and maximizing your exit value.
